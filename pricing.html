FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is a Sponsored Pin?

A Sponsored Pin puts your business on Leo's map. When visitors are nearby, Leo recommends you by name with your story, menu, and context — like a personal recommendation, not an ad.

Do I need to download an app?

No. La Voz PR runs in your mobile browser. Just scan a QR code or visit lavozpr.com, enter your code, and Leo is ready to talk.

Does Leo speak Spanish?

Yes! Leo is fully bilingual — English and Spanish with authentic Puerto Rican flavor. He switches naturally based on how you talk to him.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Sponsored Pins are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Visitor passes are one-time purchases that expire after their duration.

What are Bulk Codes?

Hotels, Airbnbs, and tour operators can buy access codes in bulk at a discounted rate. Give your guests Leo as a welcome amenity — co-branded with your business.

How do Event Packages work?

We map your entire venue — every stage, vendor, service area — and Leo becomes the official voice navigator for your event. Attendees scan a QR at the entrance and they're set.
